What is Soft Washing?

Soft washing is a cleaning method using low pressure and specialized solutions to safely remove mildew, bacteria, algae, and other organic stains from roofs and other building exteriors. It is better for cleaning wood and vinyl surfaces as it prevents damage to the material.

What is Pressure Washing?

Pressure washing or power washing is the use of high-pressure water spray to remove loose paint, mold, grime, dust, mud, chewing gum, and dirt from surfaces and objects such as buildings, vehicles, and concrete surfaces. It is better for cleaning concrete and stone surfaces as it provides a deep clean.
